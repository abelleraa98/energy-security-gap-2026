# energy-security-gap-2026

## Project Overview

This project evaluates how different nations are responding to the 2026 Middle East energy crisis. By analyzing global trade flows, strategic reserves, and renewable energy adoption rates, this analysis identifies which countries are successfully "decoupling" from oil and which remain highly vulnerable to supply shocks. 

## Objectives

* **Assess:** Calculate an 'Exposure Index' for major economies based on Middle East oil reliance.
* **Monitor:** Track the acceleration of renewable energy capacity post-February 2026.
* **Evaluate:** Determine the effectiveness of national emergency energy policies in reducing oil consumption.
